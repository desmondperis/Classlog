About Class Log

Class Log is a class-coverage and lesson-logging tool built for Mount Carmel School. It gives teachers a quick way to record what they actually taught, and gives school leaders a clear picture of coverage across classes and departments.

What teachers can do

  • Record the topic covered in each class, period by period, through the week.
  • Note substitutions, duties (assembly, extended slot, break), and other work.
  • Plan topics ahead of time.
  • Optionally add their weekly classes and duties to their own Google Calendar, with reminders.

What heads of department and administrators can do

  • Review coverage across the school by day, week, or month.
  • Set timetables, duties, and planned topics.
  • Export coverage summaries.

Teachers sign in with their Google account. The app is used only by Mount Carmel School staff, and access to school-wide data is limited by role.
