Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Class Log is a tool used by staff of Mount Carmel School to record the topics they cover in class and, optionally, to add their timetable and duties to their own Google Calendar. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Information we access

  • Your Google account name and email address — used only to sign you in and to label the records you create.
  • Your Google Calendar (with your permission) — the app can create, update, and delete events on your own calendar, used only to add or remove the classes and duties you choose to sync. Class Log does not read your existing calendar entries for any other purpose.

How your data is used and stored

  • Your class logs, timetable, and duty records are stored securely (on Cloudflare) solely to provide the service to you and your school.
  • Class Log does not sell your data, does not share it with third parties, and does not use it for advertising.
  • Access to school-wide coverage is limited to authorised heads of department and administrators of your school.

Use of Google user data

Class Log's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Removing access

You can revoke Class Log's access to your Google account at any time from your Google Account permissions page.

Contact

For any questions about this policy or your data, contact desmondperis@gmail.com.
